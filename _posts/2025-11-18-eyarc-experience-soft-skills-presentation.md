This assignment covers how to prepare for a presentation, use effective body language and verbal delivery, and leave your audience with a memorable message.

**Learning objectives**
1. Prepare with clear notes and tailored content that connects with your audience.
2. Use confident body language to support your message.
3. Speak clearly with good tone, pacing, and fewer filler words.
4.Use repetition and storytelling techniques to improve memorability.

**Suitable courses:** Designed for any class that talks about public presentations
