Welcome to the Advanced GenAI topics assignment! In this module, we will dive deep into some of the advanced techniques of generative AI (GenAI), exploring how these innovative models go beyond simple content generation to tackle complex, specialized tasks. You will learn about the newest techniques for improving GenAI models, from pre-training and fine-tuning to more advanced methods like retrieval-augmented generation and multi-agent systems. Whether you are interested in text generation, image creation, or real-time decision-making, this lesson will bring you one step closer to harnessing the full potential of GenAI, specifically in an accounting and finance context. Let’s get started!

**Learning objectives**
1. Demonstrate an understanding of advanced GenAI techniques in accounting and finance.
2. Evaluate the benefits and challenges of implementing GenAI techniques.
3. Propose GenAI enhancement solutions to complex accounting and financial challenges.

**Suitable courses:** AIS, Business, Data Analytics
