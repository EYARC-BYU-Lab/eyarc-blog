While net income is widely considered to be the single most important accounting metric, revenue is a close second. To really understand a company’s top-line revenue, decision-makers need to understand how the company’s various segments contribute to the company’s overall revenue. In Mini Case 3, students use data analytics dashboards to gain insight into which product segments of a company drive the company’s revenue and profitability.
