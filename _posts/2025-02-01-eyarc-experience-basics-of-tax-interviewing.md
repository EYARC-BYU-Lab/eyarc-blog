In this assignment, you’ll get an overview of tax interviewing and learn how to effectively prepare for tax interviews. Preparing includes gathering needed documents, understanding the client’s situation, studying relevant tax laws, then reviewing interpersonal skills and effective inquiry skills.

**Learning objectives**
1. Define the purpose and significance of tax interviews in tax accounting.
2. Prepare to conduct effective tax interviews.
3. Practice professionalism when discussing challenging tax situations.

**Suitable courses:** Tax
