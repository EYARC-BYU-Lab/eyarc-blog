Welcome to the Physical Inventory Counts assignment. In this assignment, you’ll get an overview of physical inventory counts, practice with mini assignments, and explore real-world examples of how these counts impact audits.

**Learning objectives**
1. Explain the significance of inventory counts in ensuring accurate financial reporting and inventory management.
2. Describe the components of the audit risk model and explain their relevance to the audit of inventory.
3. Perform physical inventory count procedures, such as sheet-to-floor and floor-to-sheet tests, to verify inventory accuracy.
4. Analyze the impact of technological advances—such as barcodes, RFID tags, drones and artificial intelligence—on inventory counts and their implications for management and auditing processes.

**Suitable courses:** Audit
