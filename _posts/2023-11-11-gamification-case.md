This case asks students to assume the role of a chief technology officer and decide whether the information technology compliance training for their organization should be changed to a gamified training model from a non-gamified training model. The real-world data sets are smaller and represent survey and experimental data. This case is offered in Tableau and is accompanied with how-to videos.

**Suitable courses:** Cost or managerial accounting; Accounting information systems; Data analytics
