This assignment introduces some of the fundamentals of management accounting, focusing on how data analytics can enhance profitability in the dining establishments of a resort, Serenity Now. While the resort and the cost data are fictitious, the sales data is from a real organization and has been anonymized. Students will analyze dashboards, identify and analyze trends, and develop strategic recommendations.
 
To accomplish this, students will utilize preconfigured Tableau dashboards to interpret data from Serenity Now. Tableau is a business intelligence and data visualization tool that helps users share insights from data through interactive visual dashboards and reports.
 
**Learning objectives**
1. Develop and apply an analytical mindset to address managerial accounting challenges.
2. Interpret data visualizations and dashboards to identify trends and support strategic decision-making.
3. Prepare professional recommendations based on financial and operational data analysis.



**Suitable courses:** Introductory Managerial, Data Analytics
