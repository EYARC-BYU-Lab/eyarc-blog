This two-part case guides students through the exploration of generative AI technology while emphasizing the importance of using it ethically. The case has students complete a critical analysis of generative AI’s potential uses in various accounting domains and consider strategies for its ethical implementation.

The case:
* Highlights the tension between the innovative potential of generative AI and the ethical challenges it presents. 
* Encourages students to think holistically about the implications of generative AI integration in accounting practices.
* Equips students with the tools to ethically navigate the evolving landscape of generative AI in business. 

We recommend faculty couple this case with the EYARC Ethics Mindset materials that are posted on the EYARC website. Portions of the EYARC Ethics Mindset are included as an appendix in this case.

This case is tailored for any business or accounting course.

**Suitable courses:** AIS, Audit, Data Analytics, Financial, Managerial
