You intern on the research team at Summit Advisory, a consulting firm. A client, Sunbeam Energy, has asked your team to investigate one of five financial reporting issues that could affect their business. Specifically, they are asking about the following topics:

- Stock-based compensation liability awards
- Lease modifications
- Sale and leasebacks
- Remeasurement of assets and liabilities denominated in a foreign currency
- Noncontrolling interests

Your task is to gather credible information on one of these topics, evaluate different sources, learn how to prepare a technical memo and write a memo your team can use to make recommendations.

**Learning objectives**
1. Locate and interpret relevant accounting guidance using the FASB Codification and other authoritative sources.
2. Analyze and evaluate how accounting codification is applied in real-world corporate filings, such as 10-Ks, by identifying recognition, measurement, and disclosure practices in real filings.
3. Compose an original accounting memo that integrates codification requirements, scholarly research, and company-specific applications.
4. Incorporate feedback to improve clarity, accuracy, and critical analysis.
