This case asks students to assume the role of a data analyst for IntegrateCo, a company that installs and services integrated building management systems. The task is to perform various analyses on payroll data. The real-world data for this case includes payroll transaction and human resource data. The analytics solutions are offered in Access and Excel.

**Suitable courses:** Cost or managerial accounting; Accounting information systems; Auditing (external or internal); Data analytics
