A scenario where the student assumes the role of the new controller of the Prescott Company, a commercial real estate developer, and must make a judgment about the potential refinancing of its mortgage loan.

Part I: students define the issues relative to the scenario

Note: This application scenario requires students to apply more judgment in defining the issue and less in the remaining process steps as typical for most other application scenarios because multiple issues are present. As a result, this is why the scenario is broken into three parts so that the issues can first be identified before proceeding forward with the remaining process steps.
