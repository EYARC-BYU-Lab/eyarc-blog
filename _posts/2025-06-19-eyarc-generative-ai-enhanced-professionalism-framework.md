Description: In today’s rapidly evolving technological landscape, generative AI (GenAI) stands out as a transformative force with the potential to reshape professions, including accounting. In light of these rapid technological advancements, considering how best to train talent for the future workforce with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the artificial intelligence (AI) future is essential. The EYARC Generative AI enhanced professionalism framework is designed to provide university faculty with resources to prepare the future workforce for the opportunities and challenges presented by GenAI. The framework include a competency matrix and a curricula map to help educators find the right materials for their course.

**Suitable courses:** Various
