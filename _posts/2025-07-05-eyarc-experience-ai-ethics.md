This assignment provides an in-depth look at AI ethics, focusing on key principles and the ethical challenges professionals encounter as AI reshapes industries and society. As students explore these principles, they'll have the opportunity to apply them to real-world scenarios, helping them think critically about ethical dilemmas that arise in AI. By practicing these decisions in a low-stakes setting, students build the confidence and skills needed to approach complex ethical issues in their future careers, helping them act in ways beneficial to their profession and society.

**Learning objectives**
1. Understand key ethical principles in AI
2. Apply ethical reasoning to AI dilemmas
3. Evaluate ethical decisions using the E.T.H.I.C. framework

**Suitable courses:** AIS, Data Analytics, Business
